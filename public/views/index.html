An Apartment Hunting Platform for students in Munich that puts people first
Aparata

This first prototype of Aparata was developed in a five day hackathon at the Munich Center for Digital Technology and Management (www.cdtm.de ). CDTM is a joint institution of Ludwig Maxmilian University and the Technical University Munich and a Center for interdisciplinary research, education and entrepreneurship.
